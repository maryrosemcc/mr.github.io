Salax

2024 | 13 x 7 x 13 in | projector, super 8 film, salt, ink, fabric, geranium and winterleaf oil, glue, shell, polymer clay

"In ancient Rome, a man in love. I started as a supersition."

A broken Super 8 projector mutated into a creature soaked in salt and ink.

Salax can be experienced through 5 senses: touch, scent, taste, sight, and sound. This sculptural piece experiments with the material transformation of Super 8 film, along with the purpose of waste and the expanded possibilities of a filmic work.

What can become of the broken?
