This website is a work-in-progess. I am still in the process of cataloguing my creative projects (and creating projects for audiences other than myself).

I currently work as a Studio Assistant for an experimental filmmaker.

BA from New York University, Gallatin School of Individualized Study in 2020 with a minor in French studies. My academic concentration focused specifically on the ways visual culture shapes Empire and vice versa, through a historical grounding in the second wave of French colonialism.

Analog and digital experimental filmmaking encompass my main domain of artistic interest, along with some other miscellaneous creations. I do use ink to write, but don't be mislead by my domain name (it was only 2 bucks!).
