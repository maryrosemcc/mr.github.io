Mary Rose McClain --- NYC


This website is a work-in-progess. I am still in the process of cataloguing my creative projects.

I currently work at an arthouse cinema in Manhattan, managing and designing visuals for the theater.

BA from New York University in the Gallatin School of Individualized Study in 2020 with a minor in French studies. My academic concentration focused specifically on the ways visual culture shapes Empire and vice versa, through a historical grounding in the second wave of French colonialism.

My artistic mediums include analog and digital experimental filmmaking, painting, cross-genre writing, textiles (sewing by hand and machine), and clay (primarily polymer, sometimes stoneware).

I am currently interested in representing / picturing compositions and textures that evoke otherworldliness.
Other general themes/practices I examine in my creative work at the moment include:
  • relationship / power dynamics between artistic work and audience
  • translation
  • surreal, collagelike, and multipartite forms of storytelling
  • outerspace, futurity, (anti)neocolonialism
My full CV and graphic design portfolio are available upon request.

I designed/created/coded all elements of this website with the exception of the font.
