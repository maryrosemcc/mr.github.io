moving image miscellaneous me

July (pieces of my sleep #3)



2023 (Super 8 transfer to Digital, 2'24")


Shot at various shorelines between the Rockaways, New York and Mystic, Connecticut.

Dream-like otherworldliness inching towards you to wake you up or lull you back to sleep. Slow and serious shoreline play.

to take things in? to consume? to capture them?

Stretched and repitched sounds of waves, film projectors, and Erik Satie's Gymnopedie No. 3.
